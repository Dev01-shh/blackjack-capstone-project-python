1. Deck is unlimited 
2. No jokers
3. King/queen/jack count as 10 
4. ace can be counted as 10 or 1 
5. Cards have equal probability to be drawn 
6. Crads are not removed from the deck as and when drawn 
7. The computer is the dealer
